Caesar Salad

A Caesar salad is a green salad of romaine lettuce and croutons dressed with lemon juice, olive oil, egg, Worcestershire sauce, anchovies, garlic, Dijon mustard, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper.

How to make Caesar Salad

Ingredients
  • 1 large or 2 small heads of romaine lettuce
  • Parmesan cheese, shredded or shaved
  • Crisp croutons – homemade can be made several days ahead. The recipe below makes enough for 2 salads.
  • Caesar salad dressing – homemade is best and here is our favorite store-bought dressing in a pinch
Recipe
  1. Preheat oven to 350˚F. Cut the baguette in half lengthwise through the top of the baguette then slice diagonally into 1/4" thick pieces. Place the breads onto a baking sheet.
  2. In a small bowl, combine 3 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil and 1 tsp of finely minced garlic. Drizzle the garlic oil over the croutons and sprinkle the top with 2 Tbsp grated parmesan cheese.
  3. Toss until evenly coated. Spread in a single layer over the baking sheet and bake at 350˚F until light golden and crisp (10-12 minutes), or to desired crispness.

Greek Salad

Greek salad or horiatiki salad is a popular salad in Greek cuisine generally made with pieces of tomatoes, cucumbers, onion, feta cheese, and olives and dressed with salt, Greek oregano, and olive oil.

How to make Greek Salad

Ingredients
  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 English cucumber, cut lengthwise, seeded, and sliced 0.25-inch thick
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 cups halved cherry tomatoes
Recipe
  1. Make the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, vinegar, garlic, oregano, mustard, salt, and several grinds of pepper.
  2. On a large platter, arrange the cucumber, green pepper, cherry tomatoes, feta cheese, red onions, and olives.
  3. Drizzle with the dressing and very gently toss. Sprinkle with a few generous pinches of oregano and top with the mint leaves. Season to taste and serve.
Nutrition
  • Calories: 190
  • Protein: 4 grams
  • Carbs: 8 grams
  • Fat: 16 grams
  • Dietary Fiber 5.8g. 23%
Pasta Salad

Fresh and easy pasta salad packed with crisp vegetables, fresh mozzarella, and tossed with a simple homemade dressing.

How to make Pasta Salad

Ingredients
  • 1 pound dried pasta like fusilli, penne, rotini or farfalle
  • 1 cup sliced bell pepper
  • 1 cup thinly sliced zucchini
  • 1 cup halved cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cup (6 ounces) fresh mozzarella balls, chopped
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons juice from pepperoncini jar, optional
Recipe
  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook until tender, 6 to 10 minutes (check the package for recommended cook time). Drain and rinse well under cold water.
  2. While the pasta cooks, in the bottom of a large bowl, make the dressing. Whisk the red wine vinegar, salt, pepper, oregano, pepperoncini juice (if using), and the olive oil until blended. Add the drained and rinsed pasta to the dressing and mix well.
  3. Stir in the bell pepper, zucchini, tomatoes, scallions, pepperoncini (if using), olives, parmesan, mozzarella, and the herbs (if using). Taste for seasoning and adjust with salt and pepper as needed. Serve or for the best results, cover and refrigerate at least 30 minutes and up to 5 days.
Nutrition
  • Calories: 269
  • Protein: 4 grams
  • Carbs: 43 grams
  • Fat: 7.5 grams
  • Dietary Fiber 5.8g. 23%
